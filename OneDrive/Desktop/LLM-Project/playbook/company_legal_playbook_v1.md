CLAUSE: Limitation of Liability
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
COMPANY POSITION (as vendor):
  Cap must equal 12 months of fees paid in the prior 12 months.
  Carve-outs: IP infringement, fraud, willful misconduct — uncapped.
COMPANY POSITION (as customer):
  Cap must equal 2x total contract value.
  Carve-outs: data breach, gross negligence — uncapped.
NEVER ACCEPT: Mutual caps below 1x fees. Uncapped liability on our side.
RISK IF MISSING: CRITICAL

CLAUSE: Indemnification
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
COMPANY POSITION: Mutual indemnification only.
  We indemnify for: our IP infringement, our gross negligence.
  They indemnify for: their IP infringement, their gross negligence.
NEVER ACCEPT: Unilateral indemnification in their favor.
  Broad "arising out of" language without carve-outs.
RISK IF MISSING: HIGH

CLAUSE: IP Ownership
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
COMPANY POSITION: We retain all IP in our platform, tools, and pre-existing work.
  Customer owns custom deliverables built solely for them.
  We retain a license to use anonymized data for product improvement.
NEVER ACCEPT: Broad assignment of all work product to customer.
  Customer ownership of our background IP.
RISK IF MISSING: CRITICAL

CLAUSE: Termination for Convenience
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
COMPANY POSITION: Mutual right to terminate with 30 days written notice.
  Customer owes payment for all work completed through termination date.
NEVER ACCEPT: Customer-only termination right with no notice period.
  Termination without payment for completed milestones.
RISK IF MISSING: HIGH

CLAUSE: Data Privacy & Security
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
COMPANY POSITION: Must include DPA if processing personal data.
  Breach notification within 72 hours (GDPR standard).
  Data deletion within 30 days of contract termination.
NEVER ACCEPT: Absence of DPA when personal data is processed.
  Liability for customer's own data governance failures.
RISK IF MISSING: CRITICAL (regulatory exposure)
